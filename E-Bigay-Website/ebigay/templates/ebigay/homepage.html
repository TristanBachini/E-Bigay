What are we?

E-Bigay is an online community pantry that supports citizens who is in need of daily supplies during this pandemic. As this program alters the community pantry we all know, and lessen the contact of the public, we help riders on transferring the goods to each households applied. We accept any kinds of assistance you may, and donate those to whoever needs it. In this hard times, we need to be as one.
