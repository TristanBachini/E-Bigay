Our Mission:

E-bigay's mission is mainly to operate a community pantry in an online setting.

Our Vision:

E-bigay's envision a community in which the most vulnerable people will have the power to lift themselves in these hard times.

Core Values

We maintain the highest standard of professional and ethical behavior, value transparency and honesty in our communications, relationship and actions through our online setting community.
We value and celebrate the unique and diverse talents, experiences and perspectives of everyone, and treat our participants, partners, donors and each other with sensitivity and respect.
We hold ourselves accountable for the quality and lasting results of our work and for the commitments we make to our participants, partners, donors and each other.
We go beyond conventional ideas and approaches, so new possibilities and innovation can flourish to ensure real and lasting positive changes.
